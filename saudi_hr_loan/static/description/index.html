Employee Loan Management

In Case of financial crisis, employee can get loan from employer.

Loan installment will be automatically deducted from employee salary.

Employee Loan Request

Employee can put request for loan with details of Loan Amount, Deduction Amount, Loan Payment Start Date, Payment Duration(month).



Employee Loan Calculation

Employee can choose different EMI option and based on that system will calculate installment amount.



HR Department Approval

After confirmation, HR department will approve or refuse loan request.



Finance Department Approval

After confirmation from HR Department, Finance department will decide to Approve/Refuse Loan.



Installment Deduction in Payslip


Current month loan EMI(Installment) will automatically deducted from employees payslip.



Installment History


History of installment will be manage on employee loan record. After every installment system update the amount to pay.



Loan Completed


If employee paid all installment then system complete his/her loan and update status as completed. In any case Finance Manager can also do this manually.



Loan Skip Installment


In some case employee wants to skip any installment for particular month then employee can put skip installment request. If HR department and finance department approve this request then system skip the installment and not deducted amount from salary.

Air Allowance Calculation

Based on Employee Nationality, City and Dependent Family Members company will provide air fare allowance.

Air Fare Configuration

We need to add Air fare for city wise. According to Adult fare amount, Child fare (80%) and Infant fare (12.5%) is calculated.



Employee Dependent Information

HR Manager need to add employee's dependent information. Like Name(as in passport), member relation with employee.



Allowance information on employee contract.


If we need to give allowance to employee then we need to select Air allowance and also need to select Destination. Based on that and dependent information system will calculate amount and apply on Payroll.



Air Allowance calculation in employee payslip


According to air allowance rule, for first 3 month of date of joining air allowance amount is not provided. After that each month employee will get air allowance.

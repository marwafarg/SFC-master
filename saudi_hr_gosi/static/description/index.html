Saudi HR: GOSI Contribution

The General Organization for Social Insurance (GOSI) is a Saudi Arabian government agency concerned with social insurance in the country.

Calculations for the GOSI are based on earning of an employee and employee's nationality.

Employee GOSI Detail

We need to create record for each employee with GOSI number.



Payslip Calculation

When HR Manager will generate payslip system will automatic calculate GOSI if GOSI detail exist on employee personal information.


According to GOSI Government rules and employee nationality system will calculate the amount.



Applied Amount on Employee record.


As per GOSI rules, system will deduct amount from employee's salary which employee nationality is Saudi Arabia and Bahrain.



Company Contribution Report


According to rules, Company will also contribute some amount based on employee nationality and gives to GOSI Organization. We can get Country wise information i.e. For Saudi, Non Saudi and Bahrain.



You can print report for multiple or single record.



We can get the information for specific time period.
